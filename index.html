A Decade of Relentless Ascent

Wadhwa began his fitness journey in 2014 as a physique competitor, transforming himself from 36 kg to a nationally recognized athlete. Over the next decade he became known for precision coaching, scientific programming, and outcome-focused transformations.

He has delivered more than 15,000 transformations and is trusted by CEOs, high-net-worth individuals, celebrities, athletes, and public figures.

Before founding FastFitHub, Wadhwa served prominent roles at HealthifyMe — Consultant Fitness Expert, Quality Analyst, Subject Matter Expert, and Top Coach until 2023.
